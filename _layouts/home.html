About me

I am an affiliated researcher supervised by Prof Prof.Biwei Huang(Assistant Professor in EECS,UC San Diego) and Prof Kun Zhang(Associate Professor in CMU). Prior to leaving New York, I was fortunately supervised by Prof.Bryant Moy at NYU Center for Data Science and Prof.Michele at Grossman School of Medicine at NYU Langone. I received my master's degree jointly offered by Tandon School of Engineering and Courant Institute of Mathematics.

My research interests are focused on theory in Probabilistic Graphical models and Applied Algebraic Geometry; Causal Representation Learning in cancer-driving mutation and genomics.


Location: Charles River, Cambridge,Massachusetts, United States.
